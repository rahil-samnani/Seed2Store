Organic Wheat

Scientific Name – Triticum aestivum

Local Name– Gehun/ Common Wheat/ Bread Wheat/Soft Wheat

Wheat is the second most important crop among the cereal crops. Also known as common wheat or bread wheat. The Wheat Grain Seeds, being a staple food is used in the making of flour for leavened, flat, and steamed bread, cookies, cakes, breakfast cereal, etc. Wheat grains are packed with nutrients, including protein, fiber, B vitamins, antioxidants, and trace minerals (iron, zinc, copper, and magnesium). A diet rich in whole grains has been shown to reduce the risk of heart disease, type 2 diabetes, obesity, and some forms of cancer.
